## Goal?

The vision of WyseOS is to create a **simple**, **powerful** and **flexible** Multi-Agent Framework. It is designed to support best engineering practices for modular development, enabling developers to create robust, efficient, and adaptable applications by leveraging comprehensive mechanisms provided by the platform.
